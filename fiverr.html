Why choose thrift plan?

Access to saving products

Promoting increased saving solutions in the region to encourage saving rates with enhanced return opportunities.

Financial security

The key to a happy life today is the knowledge that there is security in tomorrow. Life can be unexpected, whether it is buying a house, renovation or a broken car. Flexible savings allow individual to live with the security that when the unexpected happens there are resources to rely on.

Enhanced return potential

Allowing employees to benefit from enhanced returns through combining individual contributions with employer matching to reward performance and loyalty as well as offering investment opportunities with renowned local funds.

Increase fininacial literacy

Increasing awareness of saving products and how to manage finances and assets to allow individuals and companies to make the most out of their income.
